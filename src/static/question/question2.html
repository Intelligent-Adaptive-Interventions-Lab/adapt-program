Ticket Price Calculation

You are designing a ticket pricing system for a theme park. The ticket price for a person is determined based on their age. The rules for ticket pricing are as follows:

  • Seniors (65 and over) pay $4.75 per ticket.
  • Kids (12 and under) pay $4.25 per ticket.
  • Everyone else pays $7.5 per ticket.

Function Signature:


        def ticket_price(age: int) -> float:

Parameters:

  • age (int): The age of the person for whom the ticket price needs to be calculated.

Returns:

float: The ticket price for the person based on their age.

Examples:


        ticket_price(68)
        # Output: 4.75

        ticket_price(10)
        # Output: 4.25

        ticket_price(25)
        # Output: 7.5
